TruthLens uses DistilBERT (distilbert-base-uncased) — a transformer model fine-tuned on 131,163 articles with 91% test accuracy. Analysis follows the exact pipeline from your training notebook (V2__DL_Model_Training.ipynb).

Pipeline: title + text → content → basic_clean() → lowercase → remove URLs → remove HTML → [^a-z\s] removed → DistilBertTokenizerFast → MAX_LEN=128 · truncation=True → softmax · {0→True, 1→Fake} → 60% threshold
Step 1 — Combine title + article text into content
Step 2 — basic_clean(): lowercase + strip
Step 3 — Remove URLs (https?://\S+ | www.\S+)
Step 4 — Remove HTML tags <.*?> and non-alpha chars [^a-z\s]
Step 5 — DistilBertTokenizerFast · padding=max_length · MAX_LEN=128
Step 6 — DistilBERT inference → softmax → label_map {0→True, 1→Fake}
Step 7 — Apply 60% threshold → Real / Fake / Uncertain
